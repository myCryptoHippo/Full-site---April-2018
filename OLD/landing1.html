What is cryptocurrency?

You've probably heard of it before, but what is it?

A cryptocurrency is a completely digital, decentralized currency running on blockchain technology. It is not controlled by any central figure, like a bank or government, and ensures complete anonymity. It is revolutionizing the way we exchange money.
