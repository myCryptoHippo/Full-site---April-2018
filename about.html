Our Mission

Cryptocurrency and blockchain technology saw an exponential increase in 2017 with Bitcoin, but most people don't know why or even what Bitcoin is. The technology behind cryptocurrency, the blockchain, is revolutionizing the world of tech and is only further projected to grow. We aim to make blockchain technology easy to learn and investing in cryptocurrencies less intimidating.

Who are we?

myCryptoHippo is a company created as part of the Junior Achievement Central Ontario Company Program. The team is comprised of crypto-curious high school students and blockchain enthusiasts. We strongly believe in the power of education in empowering the public to adapt the mainstream use of blockchain and cryptocurrencies.
