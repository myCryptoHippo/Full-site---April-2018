We are a platform created by a group of cryptocurrency and blockchain enthusiasts, for others that were in the same position we were. We aim to make it easy to learn about trading cryptocurrencies and the technology behind them through the myCryptoHippo market simulator. Check out our commercial!

How does it all work?

A gamified simulator

myCryptoHippo uses a gamified cryptocurrency market simulator. From leaderboards to CryptoCup, we'll keep you on your toes!

Compete with users globally

Our platform allows you to compete with users around the WORLD to become the richest hippo of the herd! Our CryptoCup is also global.

Control your investments

Make changes to your portfolio at your will, like a real crypto investor! Bull market? Buy, buy, buy! Beat? Sell, sell, sell! (we'll explain the terms!)

Highly Secure
